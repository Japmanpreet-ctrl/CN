Exponential Backoff

Nodes compete to use one shared communication channel. When several nodes transmit together, a collision happens and each waits for a random backoff interval before trying again.

Backoff Rule

Legend

Blue nodes are idle or waiting to send.
Green dots show data moving from sender to receiver.
Purple receiver glows when a packet arrives.
Orange ACK returns from the receiver to the sender.
Red flash at the center marks a collision.
Orange text above a node shows its countdown slots.
